Chicken Rice

Easy to make homemade hainanese chicken rice which you do not need an oven

Ingredients

  • Whole Chikcn
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chilly Flakes
  • Soya Sauce
  • Coriander
  • Spring onion
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • 5 cups of rice
  • Lemon Grass
  • Sliced Cucumber

Steps

  1. Stuff the chicken with whole garlic and ginger
  2. Fill water in a container for boiling water and boil water
  3. Add about 5 spoons of salt in the boiling water
  4. Add about 5 drips of sesame oil
  5. Put in the chicken in the water and let it simmer for around 30mins to 1hour, make sure you cover the container
  6. Wash the rice and lemon grass and put it in rice cooker
  7. While simmering the chicken, remove the residues that float in the broth
  8. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it and set it aside
  9. Pour the broth into the rice cooker and then put the rice cooker on cook
  10. Making the black sauce
    1. In a bowl, put in some oyster sauce
    2. Soya Sauce
    3. Mix it well, if you want it more thicker, add in more oyster sauce
  11. Making the chilly
    1. Some chilli flakes, put it in the mixture
    2. Add in some chicken broth
    3. Blend it well
  12. Remove all the stuffed Ginger and garlic from the chicken
  13. Cut the chicken in stripes big enough to be served well
  14. Once rice is cooked, for one serving, A bowl of rice is used to serve on plate
  15. Add the chicken meat on the plate
  16. Add the cucumber
  17. On two sauce bowls, on one, serve the black sauce and the other serve the chilly
