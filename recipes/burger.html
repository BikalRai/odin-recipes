Crunchy Burger

The best, Crunchiest Burger in Town where the taste is rich and will make you have seconds!!

Munch and Crunch to the fullest with the Crunchy Burger

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Minced Chicken 250 grams
  • Half whole egg
  • 5g Salt
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Lettuce
  • 10g Mayonese
Steps
  1. Season Minced meat with Salt
  2. Coat with beaten egg
  3. Coat with Bread Crumbs
  4. Chill the Patty for 25 mins
  5. Deep Fry the patty
  6. Toast the Bun while Deep frying the patty
  7. Apply Mayonese on bun
  8. Put the lettuce
  9. Aseemble the burger
