Chickent Chilly

A quick and easy recipe to make Chicken Chilly per your taste. You can make it sweet or non sweet

Ingredients

  • Diced meat to you liking
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Chilly Flakes
  • Soya Sauce
  • Coriander
  • Spring onion
  • Onion
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Baking powder
  • All purpose Flour
  • Tomato ketchup

Steps

  1. Apply salt and pepper to the meat
  2. Mix Baking powder and flour
  3. Coat the meat in the mixture of baking powder and flour
  4. Deep fry the meat for a crunchy taste and put aside the meat
  5. Heat Up wok and apply oil
  6. Grate ginger and garlic and make it golden color in wok
  7. Add ketchup and fry abit more
  8. Quickly add the soya sauce and the oyster sauce
  9. Add in diced onion
  10. Stir for 1 min max and toss in the well cut sping onion together with chilly flakes
  11. Toss in the meat and coat it well with the sauce that we fried
  12. If you want to make it sweet, add some honey but not too much
  13. Plate it with coriander on top
